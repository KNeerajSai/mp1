How to View Your Site Like the Example (2.png)

Method 1: Browser Zoom (Quickest)

1. Open your site: http://localhost:8080
2. Press Ctrl/Cmd + - to zoom out to 25%
3. You'll see all sections as colored stripes

Method 2: Chrome DevTools

1. Open your site in Chrome
2. Press F12 to open DevTools
3. Press Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + P
4. Type "Capture full size screenshot"
5. Press Enter - it will save a full page image

Method 3: Firefox Screenshot

1. Open your site in Firefox
2. Press Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + S
3. Click "Save full page"
4. This captures the entire page as one image

Method 4: Preview Frame

Open: layout-preview.html
This shows your site in a scaled iframe
